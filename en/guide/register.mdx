## Visual guide

### 1. Start from the homepage

![Homepage sign-up entry](/assets/screenshots/iqqz-home-top.png)

On the homepage, you can see `登录` and `开始使用`. New users should click `开始使用` to open the sign-up page.

### 2. Fill out the form

![Sign-up form](/assets/screenshots/iqqz-signup-form.png)

On the sign-up page, fill the form from top to bottom:

- Username
- Password
- Confirm password
- Accept the Terms and Privacy Policy
- Click `创建账户`

## Where to sign up

You can reach the sign-up page from:

- The `开始使用` button on the homepage
- Directly at `https://www.iqqz.com/sign-up`

If you already have an account, use the `登录` link on the sign-up page to go to sign in.

## Steps

1. Open `https://www.iqqz.com/sign-up`
2. Enter a username
3. Set a password, ideally 8 to 20 characters long
4. Confirm the same password again
5. Accept the Terms and Privacy Policy
6. Click `创建账户`

## Field guide

- Username: your login name
- Password: your account password
- Confirm password: enter the same password again
- Terms of use: must be accepted before submission
- Privacy policy: acknowledged during registration

## Tips

- Pick a username you can keep using
- Avoid reusing passwords from other sites
- If submission fails, check whether the username is already taken or the passwords do not match

## After registration

After signing up, the usual next steps are:

1. Sign in on the login page
2. Open the dashboard
3. Review pricing, models, and API settings
