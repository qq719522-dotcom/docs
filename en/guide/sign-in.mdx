## Visual guide

### 1. Open the sign-in page

![Sign-in page](/assets/screenshots/iqqz-signin-form.png)

You can open it from the `登录` link in the top navigation, or go directly to:

- `https://www.iqqz.com/sign-in`

### 2. Enter your credentials

The sign-in page has two primary fields:

- Username or email
- Password

Click `登录` when you're done.

## Steps

1. Open `https://www.iqqz.com/sign-in`
2. Enter your username or email
3. Enter your password
4. Accept the terms and privacy policy
5. Click `登录`

## Field guide

- Username or email: you can use either one
- Password: your account password
- Forgot password: goes to the recovery flow
- Register: if you don't have an account yet

## Tips

- If login fails, verify the username or email first
- Then double-check the password
- If you forgot the password, use the recovery page

## After sign in

After a successful sign-in, you'll usually land in the dashboard, where you can:

1. Review models and pricing
2. Configure API access
3. Check usage and account settings
