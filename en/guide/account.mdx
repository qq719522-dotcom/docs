## Sign up

When signing up, prepare:

- A valid phone number or email address
- A secure password
- A way to receive verification messages

## Log in

After logging in, check:

- Whether the account name is correct
- Whether your contact information is still active
- Whether the important security settings are enabled

## Profile settings

Common profile fields include:

- Avatar
- Display name
- Contact details
- Preferences or saved addresses

## Security tips

- Do not reuse weak passwords
- Never share verification codes
- Change your password if you notice suspicious activity

## Forgot your password?

You can usually recover access through your linked phone number or email.

If your account has no valid recovery method, contact an admin or support team.
