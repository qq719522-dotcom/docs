## Visual guide

### 1. Open the recovery page

![Forgot password page](/assets/screenshots/iqqz-forgot-password.png)

You can get there from `忘记密码？` on the sign-in page, or open:

- `https://www.iqqz.com/forgot-password`

### 2. Enter your registered email

The page asks for the email you used when registering, then sends a password reset link.

## Steps

1. Open `https://www.iqqz.com/forgot-password`
2. Enter the email address you used during registration
3. Click `Send reset email`
4. Check your inbox for the reset email
5. Use the link in the email to set a new password

## Field guide

- Email: must be the email used for the account
- Send reset email: sends the reset email
- Register: use this if you don't have an account yet

## Tips

- Make sure the email matches the one on your account
- Check spam or junk if the email doesn't arrive
- If the email is wrong, the reset email won't reach you

## After reset

When the password is changed, go back to the sign-in page:

1. Open `https://www.iqqz.com/sign-in`
2. Enter your username or email
3. Enter your new password
4. Sign in to the dashboard
