---
title: "Quick Start"
description: "The shortest path to your first successful iqqz action."
---

## Step 1: Confirm the entry point

Open the `iqqz` website or the relevant entry point and make sure you're on the correct site and account system.

## Step 2: Log in

If you already have an account, log in.

If not, create one first and then continue.

## Step 3: Learn the home page

When you land on the home page, focus on these areas:

- Navigation bar: switch between modules
- Main content area: shows the current page
- Profile area: account status and settings

## Step 4: Complete your first action

Pick the most common function from the home page or menu and complete one full workflow.

For your first try, keep it simple and do one thing at a time.

## Tips

- If a button is unclear, hover over it to see whether there is a hint
- On mobile, menus may be collapsed into a side drawer
- If you see an error, note the exact message for troubleshooting
